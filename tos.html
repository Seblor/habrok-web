2. Reputation System

The Bot provides a "Reputation System" where users can assign "praise" or "blame" to others, creating a reputation score. Users can view their own scores, while administrators have access to member scores and history within their server. Administrators also have the option to opt-out of sharing their server's scores with other servers.

We respect user privacy. The Bot will only collect and display information related to the reputation system. Personal information will not be stored or shared without user consent.

5. Administrator Responsibilities

Administrators are responsible for moderating the use of the Bot within their servers. They should ensure that the reputation system is used fairly and that any misuse is promptly addressed. Administrators may also choose to opt-out of sharing scores from their server with other servers.

6. Global Scores

Administrators on other servers can access global scores of users who have a reputation score in their server, unless an administrator has opted out of sharing scores.
