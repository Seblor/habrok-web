Privacy Policy


Last updated: 2024-01-17 Habrok's team ("us," "we," or "our") operates the Discord bot "Habrok" (the "Bot"). This Privacy Policy outlines our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when using the Bot.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Reputation System Data

The Bot collects and processes information related to the "Reputation System." This includes but is not limited to:

  • User-provided reputation ratings (praise or blame).
  • User IDs associated with submitted ratings.
  • Reputation scores and history within servers.
1.2 Opt-Out Data

For administrators who choose to opt-out of sharing scores from their server, the Bot may collect and store this preference to respect their decision

2. Use of Information

2.1 Reputation System Operation

The collected information is used to facilitate the operation of the "Reputation System." This includes displaying reputation scores, maintaining a history of ratings, and providing users with insights into their reputation within servers.

2.2 Opt-Out Preferences

Information related to administrators' opt-out preferences is used to respect their decision not to share server scores with other servers.

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Sharing Within Servers

The Bot shares reputation-related information within the server to enable users and administrators to view and interact with the Reputation System.

3.2 Global Scores

Administrators from other servers may access global scores of users who have a reputation score in their server, excluding those from servers where administrators have opted out of sharing scores.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of the data collected by the Bot. Measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of information. We also take great care not to divulge any information that could lead to any "witch hunting" or any cyberbullying.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about these Terms, please contact us on our Discord server.
